Bioluminescent Bay in Vaadhoo Island, Maldives

Why Maldives?

The Maldives is home to a magical phenomenon where the sea turns blue at night, known as bioluminescence, where microorganisms called dinoflagellates emit a blue glow when disturbed, creating a mesmerizing and enchanting experience for those lucky enough to witness it.
